How It Works

Step 1

Follow the booking link to set up a short 15 minute call to discuss your site. and the issues.

Step 2

Once we have discussed a plan you decide your level of engagement. Most first time clients choose a one time job. This is a limited engagement to fix a specific issue. This can be anything from a white screen of death crash to an hours worth of content updates.

Step 3

We perform the work and report back. This often includes a detailed explanation of the issue root cause and how to avoid it in the future.

Step 4

Now that all the work is done you choose if you want to continue with our services. One time jobs are always available or you can choose to sign up for a monthly plan with extra benefits.
